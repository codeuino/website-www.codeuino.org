How it Works

We are making a complete free software solution for everyone.

Our project is an important part of the Free Software ecosystem. We work with other free projects to create high-quality solutions that span the entire software stack.

While the many FOSSWORLD contributors develop code, smash bugs, write documentation, and help users, the Foundation acts as a guiding hand in the process and provides resources and infrastructure. It steers releases, determines what software is officially part of the Project, and acts as the official face of the FOSSWORLD Project to the outside world, though it delegates most of its authority to specialized teams.
